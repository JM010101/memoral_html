Contribute

The Fallen Gator Registry was created to honour the classmates we lost too soon. It's a living memorial, built on respect, memory, and action. But remembrance alone isn't enough. Legacy matters.

That's why we're expanding the mission.

Your donation will now do more than preserve names and stories. It will directly support current high school students through what we call Silent Hero Missions. These are real needs, identified and hand-picked by school administration. No publicity. No spotlight. No politics. Just quiet, targeted help where it actually makes a difference.

This support may fund:

  • School supplies for students who don't have them
  • Fees for field trips or academic programs they would otherwise miss
  • Equipment for sports, arts, or trades programs
  • Participation in learning opportunities that shape confidence and future paths

These are students with potential, character, and drive who simply need a break, a boost, or a bridge.

This is how legacy becomes action.

This is how memory becomes momentum.

This is how a generation helps the next one stand taller.

By contributing to the Fallen Gator Registry, you are honouring those we lost and investing in those still finding their way.

Quiet impact. Real change. Lasting meaning.

If you give, give with purpose.

If you act, act with heart.

This is how a community looks after its own.
